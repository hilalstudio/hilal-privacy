Common questions

My streak or progress disappeared. Hilal stores everything on your device. Deleting and reinstalling the app clears local data. If something looks wrong, email us with your device model and iOS version.

The day changes at the wrong time. Turn on "Sunset Timing" in Settings to roll the Hijri day over at your local maghrib; otherwise it rolls over at midnight.

Privacy. Read our privacy policy. Short version: no accounts, no personal data, your game lives on your device.
